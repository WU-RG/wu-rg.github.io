The Hundred-Year-Old Man Who Climbed Out of the Window and Disappeared is a comedy film based on the novel of the same name. It follows Allan Karlsson, a centenarian who escapes from his retirement home and goes on an adventure with a suitcase full of money, encountering gangsters, a recluse, and a woman with a pet elephant along the way. The film was successful internationally and was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Makeup and Hairstyling. A sequel was released in 2016.
